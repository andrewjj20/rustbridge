Get Involved

There are lots of ways you can help the Clojure community become more diverse:

The Easy Things

  • Join the mailing list to follow what's going on, find out about upcoming workshops and add to the discussion.

  • Introduce ClojureBridge at your local meetup groups, especially events for women.

  • Tell the women YOU know and love about it and encourage them to attend!

  • Review the Community Docs
